Hello! 👋

My name is Vikrant (Vik) Bathala, and I am a 3rd year undergraduate student at the Georgia Institute of Technology pursuing my B.S. in Computational Media. Oh! What is Computational Media? I'm glad you asked! Computational Media is a major that explores the intersection of computer science and design/liberal arts. For example, my focus is in Artificial Intelligence and Interaction Design. TL;DR half CS, half design. My career interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction and AR/VR, with an emphasis on educational technologies and accessible technologies.

Outside of school, I love to develop my culinary skills - I love cooking, baking, eating, and anything and everything involving food. I also love singing and playing piano and occasionally dabble in composing my own music. You'll also find me playing the latest indie video games, traveling whenever possible, and spending some quality time with the people I care about.
